  3. Got Engaged L&E

    Eric had to improvise how he was going to propose to Leslie. The original plan was to spend the day hiking Mt. Tamalpais, and when arriving at a scenic view, he'd ask Leslie to marry him. But the weather wasn’t going to be accommodating…

    After a leisurely morning in Union Square, Leslie and Eric found themselves at the Apple store (where else :). At this point Eric was contemplating the possible ways he was going to pull off this proposal. They decided their next stop was going to be the SF MOMA.

    On their way towards the museum, the weather broke and the Sun came out. Leslie had the wise idea that they shouldn’t spend the only sunny time of the day inside. Thinking on his feet, Eric suggested they head towards Twin Peaks.

    Once up on Twin Peaks, after looking down on the city from the pedestrian lookout, Eric suggested that they hike to the top of one of the hills. At the top they could see the Pacific Ocean, the Golden Gate Bridge, and downtown San Francisco. Looking up at Leslie with the city behind him, Eric ask Leslie to marry him. And of course she said “Yes!”

  8. Met for the First Time L&E

    Leslie and Eric were working in the same office building on the same floor, but for different companies. Shortly after Eric moved to Boston, the two met at the floor’s communal coffee station. (That might explain how Eric became a coffee aficionado.)

    Eric kept seeing this cute girl on the floor (which of course was Leslie). And to increase his chances of running into her, he kept himself overly caffeinated and hydrated. This meant he had to make multiple trips to the communal kitchen, passing by Leslie’s office each time.

    Finally, the two ran into each other—both waiting in line to use the coffee machine. Eric knew this was his moment and that he had to seize this opportunity. Eric told Leslie that he was new to town and trying to find a permanent place to live. Leslie having moved to Boston only a year earlier could relate, and invited Eric to hang out with her and her friends.

    …Leslie gave Eric her phone number. Success!
